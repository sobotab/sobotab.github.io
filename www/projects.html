C++ AVL Tree

An AVL tree is a balanced binary search tree. Named after their inventors, Adelson-Velskii and Landis, they were the first dynamically balanced trees to be proposed. Like red-black trees, they are not perfectly balanced, but pairs of sub-trees differ in height by at most 1, maintaining an O(logn) search time.

Java Garden Design Software

Me and a team of developers from the University of Delaware designed this Garden Design Application for the Mt. Cuba Center Botanical Garden's. The goal of the software was to give residence of the U.S. Mid-Atlantic area a way to design gardens for native insect species and show how their garden has an impact on their populations.
